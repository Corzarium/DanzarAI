Forbidden Rite of Soul Sacrifice is a chaos spell that fires a projectile near your cursor, as well as additional projectiles for each enemy within range. Casting this spell deals a Chaos Damage hit to you based on a percentage of your energy shield. This includes when the spell is triggered. Unlike the original skill, this transfigured version costs life instead of mana, and scales and deals damage entirely based on energy shield, rather than both life and ES. Because it has a life cost, it is unfeasible to use this with Chaos Inoculation unless the cost is reduced by 100% through Reduced Cost of Skills or negated entirely via modifiers like the legacy modifier Non-Aura Skills Cost no Mana or Life while Focused. Forbidden Rite of Soul Sacrifice has restrictions on where or how it can drop. This item is a transfigured gem. It can be obtained through the use of the Divine Font in the Lord's Labyrinth.
