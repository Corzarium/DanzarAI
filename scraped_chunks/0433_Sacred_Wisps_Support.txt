Sacred Wisps Support is a hybrid support gem. It grants the Summon Sacred Wisps spell, which is triggered when an enemy is hit by the supported wand attack, summoning two Sacred Wisps. The Sacred Wisps are proxies with a chance to use the supported wand attack, when the player fires projectiles with it. Sacred Wisps Support can drop anywhere.
