Defiance Banner is a banner aura skill. After gaining Valour from melee combat, it can be activated, consuming all Valour and placing a banner that buffs the user and allies within an area with increased movement speed, additional chance to suppress spell damage, and a flat subtraction to damage taken from suppressed spell hits. The effect of the aura is multiplied based on the amount of Valour consumed. When the banner is placed with 50 Valour, the buff provides 48% increased Movement Speed to You and Allies, +48% chance to Suppress Spell Damage to You and Allies, and -72 Damage taken of each Damage Type from Suppressed Hits to You and Allies. Defiance Banner can drop anywhere.
