Arctic Armour is a spell that reserves mana to grant the caster immunity to freeze, a reduction to physical and fire damage while stationary, the ability to chill enemies that hit them, and to cause them to leave behind a trail of chilled ground while moving. Arctic Armour can drop anywhere.
