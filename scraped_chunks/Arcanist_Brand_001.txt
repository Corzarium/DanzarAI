Arcanist Brand is a skill gem that turns a linked spell into a trigger skill. The brand periodically triggers all linked spells in a sequence while being attached to an enemy. The following restrictions apply from the description of the support gem part of Arcanist Brand: Supports spell skills that have no reservation. Cannot support skills used by totems, traps or mines. Cannot modify the skills of minions. Arcanist Brand can drop anywhere.
