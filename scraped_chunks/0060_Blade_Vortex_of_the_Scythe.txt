Hit interval: Unlike regular Blade Vortex, Blade Vortex of the Scythe will deal a hit immediately when the blades are created. Recasting Blade Vortex of the Scythe while there's already an active blade will not cause the skill to deal a hit on cast. To optimize the damage, your cast speed should be slightly longer than the blade duration and have the blade duration be slightly higher than 0.6 seconds. Blade Vortex of the Scythe has restrictions on where or how it can drop. This item is a transfigured gem. It can be obtained through the use of the Divine Font in the Lord's Labyrinth.
