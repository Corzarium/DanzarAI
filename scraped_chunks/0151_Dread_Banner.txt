Dread Banner is a banner aura skill. After gaining Valour from melee combat, it can be activated, consuming all Valour and placing a banner that buffs the user and allies within an area with additional chance to block attack damage and life gain on block. The effect of the aura is multiplied based on the amount of Valour consumed. Dread Banner can drop anywhere.
