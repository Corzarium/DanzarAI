Ancestral Cry is a warcry skill that taunts nearby enemies and grants a buff to you and nearby allies. The buff grants additional elemental resistances and maximum elemental resistances based on the power of nearby enemies. It also causes your next strike attacks to exert, targeting two additional enemies.
