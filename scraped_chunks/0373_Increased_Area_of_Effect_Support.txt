Increased Area of Effect is a support gem. This gem will increase the radius of any areas of effect produced by the linked skill. For a list of skills that feature an area of effect, see AoE skills. If a skill has more than one area of effect, all of them will be affected. Using Increased Area of Effect with Firestorm will result in larger fireballs, but spread out over a larger area. If linked to a skill that creates a minion, totem, trap, or mine, this gem will increase the radius of any AoE skills used by the summoned ally. For instance if a Necromancer is raised as a spectre, any curses it casts will have a larger AoE. Monster auras on rare monsters are not skills, they're an inherent property of the monsters, and will not be affected by this gem. The gem will not affect the trigger radius of a trap or mine, or a totem's range. Increased Area of Effect Support can drop anywhere.
