Creeping Frost is a projectile cold spell that launches a frozen skull at a targeted area. The projectile will explode when it reaches the targeted area, or if it comes into contact with an enemy. This explosion causes initial damage and creates a Chilling Area that slows and deals cold damage over time to any enemies that enter it. This chilling area will move in the direction of nearby enemies. Creeping Frost's chilling area is not considered Chilled Ground. Enemies affected by Creeping Frost's chilling area counts as being Chilled by you. Creeping Frost can drop anywhere.
