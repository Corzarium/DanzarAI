Arrow Nova Support is a support gem which modifies forward-firing bow skills to fire a single projectile at the target location instead. When the projectile hits the ground, it fires secondary projectiles in a nova, which inherit the effects of the supported skill. Arrow Nova Support can drop anywhere.
