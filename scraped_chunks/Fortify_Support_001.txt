Fortify is a support gem. It causes supported melee attack skills to Fortify on hit, granting a stackable buff that reduces incoming damage from hits by 1% per stack up to 20 by default. Skills linked to Fortify Support only grant Fortification if the hit reaches a certain threshold. Fortify Support can drop anywhere.
