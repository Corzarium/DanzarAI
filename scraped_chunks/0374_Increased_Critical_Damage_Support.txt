Increased Critical Damage Support is a support gem that increases the amount of damage done by critical strikes scored with the linked skill. The gem will work with minions, traps, mines, and totems, assuming the summoned ally has the ability to land a critical strike. Increased Critical Damage Support can drop anywhere.
