Detonate Dead is a spell that deals fire spell damage in an area around a targeted corpse, and also makes that corpse explode, dealing fire secondary damage in the same area. Detonate Dead deals damage in two separate hits: the spell damage dealt around the corpse, and the secondary damage dealt by the corpse explosion.[1] Detonate Dead can drop anywhere.
