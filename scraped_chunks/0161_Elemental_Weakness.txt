Elemental Weakness is one of the curses that can be rolled on tier 6+ maps and vaal side areas (known as "secret_area" in spawn weight table below). The curse affects the entire area and cannot be removed. The debuff applied is not a skill, but has values equivalent to a curse from a level 1 skill gem. Its effect is not modified by increased effect of Modifiers on your (Non-Unique) Maps. It can be mitigated with the upgraded Soul of Yugul or other item modifiers that offer reduced/less effect of curses on you.
