Predator Support is a support gem for minion skills which grants the supported minions more damage. The gem also grants the player the Signal Prey skill, which can be targeted on an enemy to cause minions supported by Predator Support to focus attacking the target and gain more damage with hits and ailments against it. Signal Prey will temporarily override any Aggressive or Defensive minion behaviour during the effect. Predator Support can drop anywhere.
