Quiver and shield are not classified as weapons, but this skill cannot use quiver, due to version 3.16.0 update.[1] The explosions from projectiles can overlap. Given sufficient investment into increased area of effect, many explosions can hit the same target. The projectiles from Explosive Concoction are ground-targeted, cannot collide with enemies, and cannot pierce, fork, split or chain; only the explosion hit and do damage. Testing with 9 total projectiles showed that most projectiles will overlap when the radius is 2.0 metres (average 8.8 out of 9), and all projectiles will overlap when the radius is 2.1 metres.[2] AoE breakpoints: The base radius of the skill is 1.8 metres and follows standard area of effect calculations.
