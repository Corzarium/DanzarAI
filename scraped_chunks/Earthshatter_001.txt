Earthshatter is a melee slam attack skill. When used, it sends out multiple fissures outwards that creates a spike at the end of its path. When these spikes are hit by a slam skill or a warcry, they detonate to deal area of effect damage. The same enemy can only be hit by 1 fissure per attack, no matter how many fissures it creates. Spikes Earthshatter can drop anywhere.
