Battlemage's Cry is a warcry skill that taunts nearby enemies, grants the user and allies a critical strike chance buff that scales with nearby enemies, and exerts a number of subsequent melee attacks to trigger a supported spell. For the infobox of the gem text and mods for the support part of the gem, see Battlemage's Cry Support. Battlemage's Cry can drop anywhere.
