Brutality Support is a support gem that grants more physical damage while losing the ability to deal elemental and chaos damage. The Awakened version of this gem, Awakened Brutality Support can be found in maps. Because Brutality Support prevents non-physical damage, it also prevents poisoning with supported skills, as 0 DPS poisons are never applied. Brutality Support can drop anywhere.
