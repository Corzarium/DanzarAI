Cast on Melee Kill is a support gem. It causes linked spells to trigger when the player kills an enemy with the melee attack portion of a supported melee skill. Melee Kill: Spells can only be triggered by kills with melee attack damage. Enemies killed by damage over time (e.g. Bleeding applied by a melee hit) will not trigger supported spells.[1] Trigger gem: Cast on Melee Kill has default behaviour, see cooldown and trigger. Cast on Melee Kill Support can drop anywhere.
