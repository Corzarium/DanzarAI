Charged Dash is a channeled lightning melee skill. It projects an illusion forward, which can be steered with the cursor while it moves. Waves of area damage are released from the player to the illusion, dealing damage around the path it has traveled. Releasing the channel creates a final wave and teleports the attacker to where the illusion was. Charged Dash can drop anywhere.
