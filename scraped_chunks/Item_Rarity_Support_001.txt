Item Rarity Support is a support gem that increases the rarity of items dropped by monsters when they are killed using the supported skill. The rarity bonus from this gem applies to enemies killed by damage over time effects.[1][2] The degeneration effect that is first applied to a monster is responsible for its death[3].
