Energy Leech Support is a support gem which causes the supported skill to leech energy shield. It also grants the skill more damage while leeching energy shield or while at full energy shield. Has very good synergy with Soul Drinker Notable - allowing the player to utilize both damage multipliers simultaneously. Energy Leech Support can drop anywhere.
