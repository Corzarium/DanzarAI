Elemental Damage with Attacks Support is a support gem that multiplies the elemental damage dealt by supported attack skills. This support gem will multiply the elemental damage dealt by attacks skills, including the damage added by auras and equipment. It also includes unarmed attacks as well as ignites and secondary damage caused by supported attacks.[1] Elemental Damage with Attacks Support can drop anywhere.
