Flesh Offering is an offering spell that consumes one or more corpses, temporarily granting the player's minions swiftness. The skill consumes a corpse and up to 4 additional corpses around it. Each additional corpse extends the base duration of the skill by 1 second, making the maximum achievable base duration 9 seconds. Each player may only have one Offering active at any time. Casting an Offering skill while one is already active will cancel the older one. Flesh Offering can drop anywhere.
