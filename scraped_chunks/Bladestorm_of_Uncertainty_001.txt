Unlike the original skill, this transfigured version randomly (50% chance for either storm) creates Sand or Blood Bladestorms regardless of the player's stance, and has increased damage effectiveness. Bladestorms hit continuously while active and can trigger on-hit effects. Bladestorm of Uncertainty has restrictions on where or how it can drop. This item is a transfigured gem. It can be obtained through the use of the Divine Font in the Lord's Labyrinth.
