Generosity Support is a support gem. It increases the effect of linked non-curse auras on allies, but prevents them from affecting the player. This gem can be used to increase the effect of auras on the player's allies. Generosity can only affect auras cast by the player, and cannot support curse auras[1] or mine skills.
