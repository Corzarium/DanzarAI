Mana Leech is a support gem. Attack Skills supported by this gem will leech mana back to the player, based on how much damage they deal. Unlike most sources of mana leech that only count physical damage, the leech from this gem considers all attack damage types when determining how much mana the attacker will gain. However, it will not work on damage over time effects. Mana Leech Support can drop anywhere.
