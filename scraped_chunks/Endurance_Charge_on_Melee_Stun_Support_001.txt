Endurance Charge on Melee Stun is a support gem. When an enemy is stunned by a linked melee skill, the character will gain an endurance charge. Only one endurance charge can be gained per attack use, regardless of how many enemies are stunned. Supported skills also deal more damage per endurance charge and has reduced enemy stun threshold. Only supported skills are affected by the more damage multiplier.
