Dominating Blow is a melee attack that applies a debuff. If a non-unique enemy dies with the debuff, it consumes their corpse to summon a friendly Sentinel of Dominance minion. Sentinels copy the modifiers of the monster it was raised from. Dominating Blow can drop anywhere.
