Elemental Proliferation Support is a support gem that causes elemental ailments applied by supported skills to affect nearby enemies. The proliferation aura-like effect continues through death on intact corpses, see Elemental proliferation for the mechanism. Elemental Proliferation Support can drop anywhere.
