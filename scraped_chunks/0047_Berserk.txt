Berserk is a skill that applies a buff which grants increased rage effect. It requires rage to cast and expends rage while active. The effect lasts until you run out of rage. Using Berserk will cause you to stop losing life with Rite of Ruin as you will start to lose rage. Berserk can drop anywhere.
