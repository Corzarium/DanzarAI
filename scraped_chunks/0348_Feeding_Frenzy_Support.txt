Feeding Frenzy Support is a minion support gem. It changes the supported minion behaviour to be aggressive, allowing them to engage on enemies from farther away. Supported minions have a chance to give the owner the Feeding Frenzy buff on hit, which grants a damage and speed bonus to all friendly minions. Feeding Frenzy Support can drop anywhere.
