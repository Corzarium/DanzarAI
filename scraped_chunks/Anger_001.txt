Anger is an aura skill that adds fire damage to the attacks and spells of the caster and their allies. See the aura page for more details. The aura area of effect radius of a level 1 Anger is 3.6 metre. The area of effect scales with gem level and quality, as well as other source of increase/more area of effect modifiers. Anger can drop anywhere.
