Blastchain Mine Support is a support gem that modifies the supported spell or ranged attack skill so that instead of casting it directly, the player throws a mine which uses the skill when the player detonates them. Each mine deals more damage for each mine detonated consecutively. Blastchain Mine Support can drop anywhere.
