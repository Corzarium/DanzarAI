Frenzy of Onslaught is an attack skill that grants Frenzy Charges on hit and also gets bonuses from them. This skill requires any melee weapon to use. Frenzy of Onslaught has restrictions on where or how it can drop. This item is a transfigured gem. It can be obtained through the use of the Divine Font in the Lord's Labyrinth.
