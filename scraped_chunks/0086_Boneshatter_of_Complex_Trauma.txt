Boneshatter of Complex Trauma is a melee attack skill and also hurts you. Successive uses will raise the damage dealt both to enemies and you through Trauma stacks which reset after reaching 10 stacks. Stunning an enemy with the strike releases a damaging area of effect pulse. Boneshatter can be used with a Mace, Sceptre, Axe or Staff.
