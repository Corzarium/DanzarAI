Unlike the original skill, this transfigured version has more damage and damage effectiveness, does not trigger secondary explosions, does not convert damage, and has less trap throwing speed. Explosive Trap of Magnitude has restrictions on where or how it can drop. This item is a transfigured gem. It can be obtained through the use of the Divine Font in the Lord's Labyrinth.
